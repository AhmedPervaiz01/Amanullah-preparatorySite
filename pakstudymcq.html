Pakistan Studies MCQs

  • Who was the first President of the Constituent Assembly of Pakistan?

    • A. Liaquat Ali Khan
    • B. Quaid-e-Azam
    • C. Moulvi Tameez-ud-Din
    • D. Sardar Abdur Rab Nishtar

    Answer: B

  • What age was prescribed for President in the 1956 constitution?

    • A. 40 years
    • B. 45 years
    • C. 50 years
    • D. 55 years

    Answer: A

  • Which kind of system of Government was introduced in the 1973 constitution?

    • A. Parliamentary
    • B. Presidential
    • C. Basic Democracy
    • D. Autonomous

    Answer: A

  • How many articles are there in the 1973 constitution?

    • A. 220 Articles
    • B. 240 Articles

    • D. 280 Articles

    Answer: D
