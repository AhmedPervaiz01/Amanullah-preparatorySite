English MCQs

  • 1. What is the synonym of 'Abandon'?

    • A. Keep
    • B. Leave
    • C. Continue
    • D. Start

    Answer: B

  • 2. What is the antonym of 'Brave'?

    • A. Fearless
    • B. Bold
    • C. Cowardly
    • D. Heroic

    Answer: C

  • 3. Choose the correct spelling:

    • A. Recieve
    • B. Receive
    • C. Recive
    • D. Recieve

    Answer: B

  • 4. Which word is a noun?

    • A. Run
    • B. Blue
    • C. Happiness
    • D. Quickly

    Answer: C

  • 5. What is the plural form of 'Cactus'?

    • A. Cactuses
    • B. Cactus
    • C. Cacti
    • D. Cactii

    Answer: C

  • 6. Which sentence is correct?

    • A. She don't like apples.
    • B. She doesn't likes apples.
    • C. She don't likes apples.
    • D. She doesn't like apples.

    Answer: D

  • 7. Find the adjective in this sentence: "The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog."

    • A. Fox
    • B. Jumps
    • C. Quick
    • D. Over

    Answer: C

  • 8. What is the past tense of 'Go'?

    • A. Goes
    • B. Went
    • C. Gone
    • D. Going

    Answer: B

  • 9. What is the comparative form of 'Good'?

    • A. Gooder
    • B. Better
    • C. Best
    • D. More Good

    Answer: B

  • 10. Fill in the blank: "She has been working here _____ five years."

    • A. Since
    • B. For
    • C. At
    • D. On

    Answer: B

  • 11. What is the opposite of 'Heavy'?

    • A. Light
    • B. Large
    • C. Small
    • D. Thin

    Answer: A

  • 12. Choose the correct option to complete the sentence: "She is _____ university student."

    • A. a
    • B. an
    • C. the
    • D. none

    Answer: B

  • 13. Select the word that means 'a place where books are kept':

    • A. Library
    • B. Bookstore
    • C. Archive
    • D. Study

    Answer: A

  • 14. Choose the correct past form of 'Eat':

    • A. Eats
    • B. Eating
    • C. Ate
    • D. Eaten

    Answer: C

  • 15. What is the synonym of 'Happy'?

    • A. Sad
    • B. Elated
    • C. Angry
    • D. Furious

    Answer: B

  • 16. Choose the correct preposition: "He is interested _____ learning languages."

    • A. on
    • B. in
    • C. at
    • D. by

    Answer: B

  • 17. What is the plural form of 'Child'?

    • A. Childs
    • B. Children
    • C. Childrens
    • D. Childes

    Answer: B

  • 18. Which sentence is correct?

    • A. She can speaks English.
    • B. She can speaking English.
    • C. She can speak English.
    • D. She can speaks English

    Answer: C
