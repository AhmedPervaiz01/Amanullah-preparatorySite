Islamic Studies MCQs

  • Who was the first Caliph of Islam?

    • A. Umar ibn al-Khattab
    • B. Ali ibn Abi Talib
    • C. Abu Bakr
    • D. Uthman ibn Affan

    Answer: C

  • Which angel brought the revelations of the Quran to Prophet Muhammad (PBUH)?

    • A. Mikail
    • B. Israfil
    • C. Jibraeel (Gabriel)
    • D. Izraeel

    Answer: C

  • In which city was the Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) born?

    • A. Medina
    • B. Jerusalem
    • C. Mecca
    • D. Cairo

    Answer: C

  • How many chapters (Surahs) are there in the Quran?

    • A. 114
    • B. 99
    • C. 123
    • D. 100

    Answer: A

  • Which Surah is also known as the heart of the Quran?

    • A. Surah Al-Baqarah
    • B. Surah Al-Fatiha
    • C. Surah Yaseen
    • D. Surah Al-Ikhlas

    Answer: C

  • What is the name of the last Surah of the Quran?

    • A. Surah An-Nas
    • B. Surah Al-Falaq
    • C. Surah Al-Ikhlas
    • D. Surah Al-Kawthar

    Answer: A

  • Which Islamic month is fasting observed in?

    • A. Muharram
    • B. Rajab
    • C. Ramadan
    • D. Shawwal

    Answer: C

  • Who was the first person to accept Islam?

    • A. Abu Bakr
    • B. Ali ibn Abi Talib
    • C. Khadijah bint Khuwaylid
    • D. Umar ibn al-Khattab

    Answer: C

  • How many times do Muslims pray every day?

    • A. Three times
    • B. Four times
    • C. Five times
    • D. Six times

    Answer: C

  • What is the pilgrimage to Mecca known as?

    • A. Salah
    • B. Zakat
    • C. Hajj
    • D. Sawm

    Answer: C
